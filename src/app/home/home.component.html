Footprint is a carbon-tracking app for individuals. The user will have the option to add their vehicle or home carbon tracking. Footprint tracks the output of your carbon on a month-to-month basis. Also, you will have many opportunities to reduce your footprint through the recommendation section, which will provide links to purchase a carbon-reducing product. The tech stack used to build the project is Angular, Typescript for the Frontend, and Spring Boot Java for the Backend. I hope you enjoy it!
